The goal wasn't just "install Grafana" — it was to build a stack that mirrors what an SRE or
detection engineer would actually operate, and use it as the foundation for a security layer on
top of standard observability.

## What's running

- **Prometheus + Grafana** for metrics and dashboards
- **Loki** for log aggregation, paired with Promtail
- **ELK** for a second, comparative log pipeline
- **Falco** for runtime detection, feeding alerts that turn raw observability data into a
  detection pipeline rather than just a monitoring one

## The framing that made this click

Security-focused platform engineering isn't a pivot away from DevOps/SRE — it's a layer on top of
it. Falco rules only matter if the underlying metrics and logs pipeline is solid first.
